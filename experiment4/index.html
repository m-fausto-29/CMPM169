Experiment 4 - Images, Video, & Sound Art

Imitate

For this experiment, I mainly took inspiration from a piece by Ivan Rudnicki where they created an interactive Vinyl Turntable that produced sound when the user sets down the Vinyl and decided to create a modern take on this piece.

Integrate

Along with utilizing code that I have integrated in past experiments, I also found resources online where I combined their code to produce effects that I wanted to achieve. I also used an example from a YouTube video and p5.js as a base to build off of.

Innovate

For this section, I improved my main audio visualizer by making it radial instead, while also improving a particle system I had used before in a previous experiment to give this piece more visual appeal.

Reflection

I worked on this experiment by myself with the help of a couple online resources. I learned a lot about image and sound manipulation through this piece and will definitely refer back to this piece in the future. I had fun working on a piece with a bright aesthetic, along with incorporating one of my favorite songs as well. There were a couple of minor hiccups that I was thankfully able to resolve, but overall I am happy with the final product.
